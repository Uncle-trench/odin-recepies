Rice & Beans

This recipe is easy to make,not to mention the ingredients needed are readily available at cereal shop or market near you. Since the recipe makes the most use of the fresh ingredients it leaves one with a yearning for second helpings and a full belly filled with exquisite taste and nutirents.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Chop the onions into small pieces and add them to a pot containing some oil. Keep stirring the onions until they turn to a brown color.
  2. Once the onions are cooked you can add your diced tomatoes into the mix and reduce the heat to allow even cooking of the tomatoes.
  3. As the tomatoes cook, add 2 cups of rice into a pot containing 4 cups of water, add salt and oil to your taste and place the pot on low to medium heat.
  4. Once the tomatoes are well broken down and cooked we can now add our beans into the mixture and mix it well with the tomatoes and onions. Add water until it covers the cereal and also add salt accompanied with your favourite spices.
  5. Let the beans come to a boil with regularly stirring it to make sure everything cooks properly.
  6. Check on the rice to see if the rice has absorbed all the water and its not soggy but flaky in nature.
  7. Once the beans is well cooked turn off the heat and add your cut parsley and mix it all together.
  8. Serve while hot with your favourite chilled drink of choice.

Don't be afraid of second helpings! Bon Appetite!
