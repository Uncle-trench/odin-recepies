Ugali and Eggs

This recipe has a cult following back in Kenya due to its versatility in being able to be eaten at any time of the day, be it as breakfast or lunch or even something to bite at on the go. The dish is also easy and quick to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • maize flour

Steps

  1. Cut the onions into small bits and add them to a pan contiaining oil. Let them cook until they turn into a golden brown color.
  2. Add your cut tomato to the onions and let it cook evenly.
  3. In a separate pot,boil some water for the ugali. Once the water boils, add the maize flour untill it mixes properly with the water and conctantly keep on turning and mixing it to ensure its evenly cooked.Set aside once done.
  4. Back to our pan.Once the tomato and onion are cooked add your three eggs in and continue stirring until the eggs solidifies togethr with the onion and tomato.Keep on stirring to avoid the egg sticking to the pan.
  5. Add salt together with your spice of choice and you can add some parsely also.

Serve with a side dish of your favourite greens.
